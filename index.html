// About Me

Who Am I?

I am Riadur Rahman — known online as syntax_rrk — a tech developer who has spent nearly a decade at the intersection of code, creativity, and engineering. I was born on March 10, 1999, and I am currently based in Mirpur, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I am not someone who fits neatly into a single box. I am simultaneously a Frontend Developer, a WordPress architect, an Android app developer, a graphic designer, and a blockchain enthusiast — all at the same time.

My journey started with a foundation in Electrical Engineering, where I completed my Diploma from UCEP Institute of Science and Technology. That engineering mindset — systematic thinking, hardware understanding, circuit logic — never left me. I later transferred into Computer Science & Engineering at SouthEast University, because I refuse to be confined by convention.

Since 2017, I have been deep in web development — WordPress, JavaScript, Python, blockchain, and responsive design. Through ReDesign Studio (my own creative studio, running since 2018 — where I still work as a Frontend Developer), I have delivered 150+ projects. I have also built 20+ Android apps for real clients using Java and Firebase.

More recently, I have stepped into the world of MCP (Model Context Protocol) Servers — building AI-powered automation systems using Claude AI. I have developed 7+ MCP servers for clients, including e-commerce automation (bKash, Nagad, Rocket payments), cryptocurrency trading tools connecting Binance and Bybit, and business workflow automation. I understand MCP architecture at about 85% depth and continue building in this space actively.
